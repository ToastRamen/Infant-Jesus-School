Health And Safety Protocols To Be Observed On The Campus

1. Gate 1 is for drop-off only; no parking is allowed. It will be used as an entrance for students. Gate 2 will be used as an exit and for pick-up only. After dismissal, students are not allowed to roam around or stay on campus. Students may stay in covered court while waiting for someone to pick them up.


2. Upon entering the school, wearing a face mask is a must for everyone. Each student should undergo thermal scanning of body temperature. Students with a body temperature of 37.5°C or above shall proceed to the holding area for further verification.


3. If body temperature remains high accompanied by other symptoms, the student shall be brought to the School Clinic or Isolation Room. Parents shall be contacted to fetch the student. The clinic staff shall further coordinate with the student’s parents for contact tracing if necessary.


4. Physical distancing of at least 1 meter must be observed on the campus. Follow the markers for directions and proper distancing.


5. Students must observe proper handwashing. To properly wash the hands, use soap and water, and rub every surface of the fingers and hands for at least 20 seconds. Students must bring their own hand towel or handkerchief.


6. Students are given a 20-minute break for snacks. All students are required to bring their own food and water container or tumbler. Water dispensers are available in the canteen area for a water refill. There is no food available in the canteen.
