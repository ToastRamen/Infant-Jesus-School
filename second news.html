New Elected COCOSA Officers

Congratulations to the new elected COCOSA Officers this school year 2022-2023!


Ma. Angel V. Rufo (President): "Leadership is not about who leads and who follows, it is working together in harmony to make big things happen."


Ken Jero M. Balad-on (Vice President): "The greatest leader is the one who is led by the Spirit of God; they lead with guidance, transformation, and empowerment!"


Freya Louise A. Barrientos (Secretary): Only those who are brave enough to take the initiative are great leaders.


Chris Andrew G. Saquita (Treasurer): Great leaders are the ones who seek to value and inspire ordinary people to strive for prodigious achievement


Sofia Nicole L. Repedro (Auditor): Leadership is about how you inspire others to become a leader of their own.


Billy Asher P. Flores (Business Manager): Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much.


Nicole Blaise M. Rafael (P.I.O): Leadership is a behavior of inspiring others, not just a power or a role.
