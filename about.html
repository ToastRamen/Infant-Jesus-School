IJS Core Values

Infant Jesus School's three-fold ideal of "Fides, Scientia, Vita" (Faith, Knowledge, Life) find expression in our core values as we Learn, Love, and Live:

Core Values
  • Living Faith

    1. We believe the Holy Spirit inspires all that we do.

    2. We embrace the Gospel of Jesus Christ and encourage a personal relationship with God the Father.

    3. We believe and promote our Catholic values.

    4. We trust in God's loving presence and providence when acting or discerning God's will.

  • Achieving Excellence

    1. We respond to challenges and pursue continous improvement thru innovation.

    2. We nurture a love of lifelong learning.

    3. We strive for academic excellence.

    4. We promote curriculum and instruction for 21st century learning.

  • Serving in Communion

    1. We prepare students for leadership and service in community.

    2. We lead through example and are accountable for our actions.

    3. We work together to achieve a common goal.

    4. We build community thru cooperation and collaboration.

Mission

United by our thee-fold ideal of "Fides, Scientia, Vita" Infant Jesus School is dedicated to educating students in a Christ-centered learning environment that fosters character formation and academic excellence for integral human development.

Vision

We are and will be a school striving to provide quality K-12 Christian education with Catholic orientation, enabling students to develop their maximum potential in an engaging, inspiring and challenging learning environment.
